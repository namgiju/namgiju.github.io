Welcome to the Gachon University International Student Portal

This page provides information about visas and residence

Visa

The D-2 visa is for students enrolled in associate, bachelor's, master's, or doctoral degree programs.
Exchange students, work-study program students, and visiting students can also obtain a D-2 visa.

Alien Registration Card (ARC)

Foreigners staying in Korea for more than 90 days must obtain an ARC.
Application must be made through the immigration office and requires documents like passport and enrollment certificate.

Change of Residence

If you change your address, you must report it within 14 days.
You can report it online through the HiKorea website or by visiting the nearest immigration office.

Following visa regulations is the beginning of a safe student life

Violations may result in legal penalties
